Vintage

Hunting Ground buys daily from 12pm–6pm, no appointment necessary. We buy seasonally and only clothing 20 years or older (1930’s–Mid 1990s-ish). In addition to clothing, we accept shoes, bags, scarves and hats. We are not currently buying vintage jewelry or suits (men’s or women’s).

How It Works:We will look through your items and determine what pieces are a good fit for our collection. All Items must be clean and in excellent condition. Please keep in mind that our merchandise is highly edited and focuses on styles that reflect current trends, so we may only accept a fraction of what you bring in. We will price items we would like to buy and you receive 30% of that price. We offer cash payment for a total buy of under $50, and a check for a buy totaling over $50.

Check our Instagram feed for current trends, or stop by our shop and get a feel for our style.
